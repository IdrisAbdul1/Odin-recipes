Chia Seed Pudding

Description

Chia seed pudding is a creamy, nutrient-packed treat made by soaking chia seeds in liquid, allowing them to absorb and expand into a pudding-like texture. The seeds act as natural thickeners, thanks to their high fiber content, creating a gel-like consistency when mixed with liquids like plant-based milk, fruit juice, or yogurt.

This dish is incredibly versatile and can be sweetened with natural options like maple syrup, agave, or blended fruit. Popular toppings include fresh berries, sliced bananas, nuts, seeds, granola, or a drizzle of nut butter. Chia seed pudding can be prepared ahead of time, making it a convenient option for breakfast, snacks, or dessert.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup chia seeds
  • 2 cups almond milk (or any plant-based milk)
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup or agave nectar (adjust to taste)
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Fresh fruit, nuts, or granola for topping (optional)

Steps

  1. GIn a mixing bowl, combine the chia seeds, almond milk, maple syrup, and vanilla extract.
  2. Whisk the mixture well to ensure the chia seeds are evenly distributed.
  3. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight, allowing the chia seeds to absorb the liquid and thicken.
  4. Once the pudding has set, give it a good stir. If it's too thick, add a bit more almond milk to reach your desired consistency.
  5. Serve the chia seed pudding in bowls or jars, topped with fresh fruit, nuts, or granola if desired.
